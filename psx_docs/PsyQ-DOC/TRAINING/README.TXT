ABSTRACT: TRAINING MATERIALS DIRECTORY			12/12/96


This directory contains a collection training seminar materials 
produced and presented by Sony Computer Entertainment Japan and 
Sony Computer Entertainment America.  The training materials have 
been divided into the time period in which they where released, 
Winter95 and Fall96.  Within the winter95 material we have two
areas, the beginners and advanced. The beginners materials is 
developed for new PlayStation developers and the advanced 
materials is for the seasoned PlayStation developers.

NOTE: The contents of this directory has just been translated 
and has not been through our technical review process.
